Exploring Asteroid Diameter and Distance from Earth in a Bubble Chart

This chart combines data from two prior charts, displaying asteroid distances from Earth and their diameters as bubbles. The x-axis, like in previous charts, ranges from 0 to the total number of asteroids for the chosen date. The y-axis represents Earth distance in megameters, with bubble size reflecting asteroid diameter. Larger bubbles indicate bigger asteroids, and their position on the chart reveals their proximity to Earth. Users can explore the relationship between asteroid size and distance from Earth.

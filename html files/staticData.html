STATICDATA

This section showcases the static data visualizations associated with my API.

Comparing Asteroid Diameters with Names in a Bar Chart

The bar chart visually shows asteroid diameters in meters with their names. The x-axis spans from 0 to the total asteroid count for the selected date. Each asteroid's name is on the y-axis, and the bar length represents its diameter. This chart simplifies comparing asteroid sizes for users.

Comparing Asteroid Distances from Earth in a Scatterplot Chart

This scatterplot chart illustrates asteroid distances from Earth in megameters, with each asteroid's name on the x-axis. The x-axis spans from 0 to the total number of asteroids for the chosen date, while the y-axis represents the distance from Earth. Users can easily see how asteroids are distributed in terms of their Earth distances. This chart helps users identify closer or more distant asteroids on the selected date.
